About Us

INFINITY FX is a pioneering multi-disciplinary new media art studio working with emerging technology to create magical experiences.

We design experiences born from our ethereal imaginations.

The aim is to amaze the audience by inviting them to become a part of a poetic, surreal, colorful and powerful digital environment.

Being a pioneer in Anamorphic Illusion 3D art installations in Turkey, our expertise stretches from public art, event design with live audio, visual, light shows to interactive experiences. By creating immersive experiences we transform spaces into multi-sensory experiences using versatile combinations of 3D projection mapping, audio, haze and LED screens to create captivating visuals for large-scale events. Infinity is an expert in offering end to end production services while also providing consultancy for conceptual design and production for multi conceptual projects.

Our passionate team values creativity, joy, speed, curiosity, an appetite for success, and eagerness to learn.
